Absolute New York
Impressive editorial work by German studio Deutsche & Japaner, who recently designed this publication chronicling the work of photographer Horst Hamann, who is best known for his black and white photos of New York City’s skyscrapers.
The stark black and white colour palette looks great and I particularly love the panoramic fore-edge print and bold typography. Most importantly though the design beautifully compliments Hamann’s own aesthetic and feels right on point.
